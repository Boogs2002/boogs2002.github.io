𓊪Arduino WaterGarbage Collector

An Arduino-based prototype designed to automatically detect and collect floating debris from the surface of the water. The system uses an ultrasonic sensor mounted on a servo motor to scan its surroundings and detect objects. Once debris is detected, multiple servo motors control the movement of the sensor, collection door, and fan mechanism to gather the waste efficiently. To ensure safe navigation, the prototype is equipped with infrared (IR) sensors that detect nearby walls and obstacles, preventing collisions while operating.
